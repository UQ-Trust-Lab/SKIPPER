Surplus income is a calculation based on your income and family size which determines how much you earn above a specific standard set by the government. The standard allows for basic living expenses, after which, half of any amount over that is paid to the Trustee in a bankruptcy. You can 'ask krieger to calculate surplus income' to calculate your specific income.
